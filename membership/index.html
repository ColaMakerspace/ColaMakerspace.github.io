Why become a member?

Membership provides you with 24/7 access to our workshop, tools, and any member-only events. As a member you get a vote for the board members and a voice in major decisions made by the organization. Your financial support goes directly to funding our mission and pays for our space, tools, and community activities.

Membership is $30 per month. Payments made through PayPal include an additional processing fee.


How to sign up

  1. Fill out the registration form.
  2. Sign-up for monthly payments via the paypal link below.
  3. Print and sign the liability waiver. Limited numbers are already pre-printed at the space.
  4. Review the Cola Makerspace code of conduct and other policies
  5. Attend a new-member orientation meeting for a tour of the space and a key-fob/key-card. Please bring your liability form for our records.
  6. Make something!

Membership Subscriptions

Membership subscriptions can be canceled at any time.
